Frequently asked questions

What counts as a "business unit"?
A business unit is any entity that connects its own data source — a retail location with its own QuickBooks, a subsidiary with its own NetSuite company, or a CSV upload for a standalone entity. One data source = one business unit. You can have multiple users accessing the same unit; users are not priced separately.
Can I mix integration types across business units?
Yes. You can have some units connected via QuickBooks, others via NetSuite, and others via CSV upload — all in the same organization. Datatrixs normalizes across all of them so you can compare units regardless of their data source.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. You can create an account and connect up to 2 business units to explore the platform. For a guided setup with your full data, we recommend booking a demo so our team can walk you through the connection and configuration for your specific setup.
How does pricing change if I add more business units?
You can add business units within your current tier at any time. If you exceed your tier's unit limit, we'll work with you to upgrade to the next tier — no automatic overages or surprise bills. Pricing is determined upfront based on your expected unit count.
Do you have annual vs. monthly billing?
Both are available. Annual billing includes a discount. Contact us to discuss what makes sense for your organization's budget cycle.
