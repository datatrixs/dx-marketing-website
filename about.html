The Future of Finance Starts Here

Datatrixs began with a simple yet powerful realization: understanding your numbers can mean the difference between success and failure. As an entrepreneur who nearly lost a business due to financial blind spots, I know this all too well. That moment of struggle became the spark for something bigger — a mission to change how businesses and CPAs work together.

Our Why

Businesses rely on CPAs and financial professionals to make sense of their data and uncover insights. But the tools they have are outdated, and the profession itself is facing a crisis — over 300,000 CPAs have left the industry in just four years. The gap is growing, and businesses are feeling the strain. That’s why we built Datatrixs: to empower CPAs and finance teams with an AI-powered platform that delivers clarity, speed, and actionable insights.

What We Believe

We believe in a future where every business, regardless of size, has access to the insights they need to thrive. A future where financial data is no longer a mystery, but a powerful tool for growth. And a future where CPAs and CFOs are equipped with the innovation they deserve to guide businesses forward.

Our Vision

At Datatrixs, we’re not just building software. We’re building a movement to transform an industry — empowering CPAs and CFOs to see the future in their data and unlock new possibilities for the businesses they serve.

The question is: what will you build with insights that truly matter?

Serge Amouzou
Founder and CEO
