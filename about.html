Our story

Built because the spreadsheet
was never the right answer

Multi-unit finance teams spend more time assembling data than analyzing it. We built Datatrixs to change that — consolidated books, instant answers, no heroic Excel work required.

Where it started

We started talking to PE-backed operators and franchise groups who were drowning in the same problem: ten QuickBooks files, one part-time controller, and a month-end close that took three weeks of copy-pasting into a master workbook.

The data existed. The problem wasn't access — it was assembly. Every insight required someone to pull it manually. By the time the report landed, the window to act had closed.

What we built

Datatrixs connects directly to your accounting systems — QuickBooks, NetSuite, Sage Intacct, CSV exports — and consolidates everything into a single financial view. No middleware, no manual exports, no reconciliation rituals.

On top of that live data layer sits Warren, our AI financial analyst. Ask a question in plain English. Get a precise, sourced answer — not a hallucination, not a generic chart. A real answer from your actual numbers.

How we work

Principles we won't compromise on

Accuracy over speed

We'd rather surface no answer than a wrong one. Warren cites its sources. Every number traces back to a transaction.

Respect for finance professionals

CFOs don't need "actionable insights." They need their actuals broken out by unit, period, and account — fast. We build for that.

Your data stays yours

We don't train models on your financial data. We don't share it with third parties. Read-only accounting connections, SOC 2 aligned.

Who we serve

Built for operators running multiple entities

We started in PE-backed retail and franchise groups. Today Datatrixs serves finance teams across retail, SaaS, biotech, professional services, and hardware — any business where the real story is only visible when you look across all your entities at once.
