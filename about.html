About Us

Empowering Entrepreneurs with AI-Driven Insights

Datatrixs was built on a simple but powerful realization: understanding your numbers is the key to business success. As an entrepreneur, I experienced firsthand how financial blind spots can make or break a company. That challenge inspired a mission—to help business owners take control of their financial data and make informed, confident decisions.

Why We Exist

Running a business means making critical decisions every day—decisions that should be based on data, not guesswork. Yet, financial insights are often locked behind outdated tools and slow processes, leaving business owners struggling to get the clarity they need. Datatrixs changes that by putting real-time, AI-powered analytics directly in the hands of entrepreneurs, helping them make faster, smarter financial decisions.

What We Stand For

We believe every entrepreneur should have access to the insights that fuel success. Financial data shouldn't be a barrier; it should be a tool that drives growth. Our mission is to simplify financial analytics, so business owners can focus on what matters— scaling their vision, optimizing cash flow, and unlocking new opportunities.

Our Vision

Datatrixs is more than just software; it's a movement. We’re transforming how businesses harness data—giving entrepreneurs and business leaders the ability to see patterns, predict outcomes, and make smarter decisions. Whether it’s streamlining operations, improving profitability, or enhancing customer experiences, our AI-powered copilot provides the insights needed to unlock new possibilities.

The question is: what will you build with insights that truly move your business forward?

Serge Amouzou
Founder and CEO
