QuickBooks Online

The most common setup for multi-unit businesses running QBO. Each business unit connects its own QuickBooks account — Datatrixs normalizes everything across all of them.

Direct OAuth — no credentials stored, no CSV exports, no middleware

Syncs P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow in under 2 minutes

Each business unit can have a different QBO company — handled automatically

Reconnect or update at any time from unit settings

Oracle NetSuite

Built for organizations already running NetSuite with multi-subsidiary structures. Datatrixs connects via API and pulls consolidated data across subsidiaries without any custom SuiteScripts.

Multi-subsidiary support built in — no custom configuration
API-based connection with token authentication
Read-only access — no write permissions required

Who this is for

Mid-market companies that have grown into NetSuite and need consolidated visibility without rebuilding their reporting stack. Finance teams managing 5–50 subsidiaries in a single NetSuite instance.

Example use case

"We have 18 subsidiaries in NetSuite. Our FP&A team was spending two weeks every quarter manually pulling subsidiary reports. With Datatrixs, they get consolidated P&Ls in the dashboard before the quarter even closes."

Sage Intacct

Native API connection — no data exports or scheduled syncs needed

Handles Intacct's dimension-based chart of accounts with automatic normalization
